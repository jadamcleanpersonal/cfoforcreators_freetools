## SECTION 3 — What it does

### what gets handled

once you connect your accounts (read-only, never moves money):

- **tells you what to save for taxes every time you get paid.** your real numbers, your state.
- **shows you what's coming in over the next 30, 60, 90 days.** across every platform.
- **tracks every brand deal.** who paid, who's late, who needs a nudge today.
- **sorts your write-offs automatically.** no more april reconstruction project.
- **answers the questions your accountant won't.** should you switch to s-corp? can you afford an editor? will a bank approve your mortgage?
- **writes the boring stuff for you.** invoices, follow-up emails, irs payments. you click send.
- **talks to you in plain english.** no jargon. no "talk to your accountant" deflection.

not a bank. not an accountant. just the money side of your channel, handled.
